Lasagna

Description

This is your classic lasagna recipe! A al dente noodles, a homemade savory tomato sauce and some real good cheese. It just doesn't get any better than that.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound pork
  • 1/2 pound beef
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 oz can of diced tomatos
  • 1 oz can of crushed tomato
  • 2 tablespoons parsley
  • 1 crushed garlic clove
  • 1 dash of sugar
  • 1 package of dried lasagna noodles
  • 1 tub of ricotta cheese
  • fresh mozzarella
  • 1 egg

    • Steps

    1. Cook the meat: Cook the ground meat in a skillet until browned and crumbly. Add the onion and continue cooking until it's translucent. Stir in the canned tomato products, half of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano, and sugar.
    2. Cook the noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until they're al dente.
    3. Cook the noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until they're al dente.
    4. Assemble the lasagna: Layer the ingredients according to the recipe (starting with sauce and ending with mozzarella) until the lasagna is assembled.
    5. Bake the lasagna: Cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven for about half an hour. Remove the foil and continue baking until the top is golden brown.
    6. In an oven preheated to 375 degrees F, this homemade lasagna should be perfectly baked in about 50 minutes (30-40 minutes covered, 5-10 minutes uncovered).
